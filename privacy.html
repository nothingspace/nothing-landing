Privacy Policy

At Nothing, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Note-taking application ("Nothing").

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • User-Generated Content: The notes, tasks, and other content you create while using Nothing.
  • Device Information: Information about your device, including device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use Nothing, including features accessed and time spent in the app.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve Nothing's functionality.
  • Develop new features based on user needs and usage patterns.
  • Troubleshoot issues and provide customer support.
  • Protect the security and integrity of our services.

Data Storage and Security

Your notes and data are primarily stored locally on your device. If you enable cloud sync, your data will be stored securely in our cloud infrastructure with encryption during transit and at rest.

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Third-Party Services

Nothing may integrate with third-party services such as Google Drive for cloud sync functionality. When you use these integrations, your data may be shared with these third parties. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third parties.

AI Features

Our AI-powered features process your notes to provide intelligent suggestions and organization. This processing typically happens on-device. For more complex AI features, some data may be processed in the cloud, but we take measures to protect your privacy during this processing.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access your personal data.
  • The right to correct inaccurate personal data.
  • The right to delete your personal data.
  • The right to restrict or object to processing.
  • The right to data portability.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at contact@nothingapp.com.

Children's Privacy

Nothing is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected personal information from a child under 13, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the app or by other means.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: contact@nothingapp.com
